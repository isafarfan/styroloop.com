Styroloop

A circular solution to EPS waste

We take it in, reduce it, reform it and plug it back into a working environment instead of sending it to landfill.

Have you ever wondered where your polystyrene goes?

EPS waste stream for households is not currently recycled. This results in approximately 10 tonnes (Or the equivalent for 45 double decker busses) of waste going into landfill or incineration every year in the UK.

Until recently the UK sent a lot of its EPS waste to China, but the waste contract is now no longer in place and as a result the need for EPS waste stream management has become a significant problem.

Our mission

Support the UK and world at large with a concept that will redirect and reduce the landfill of EPS, producing responsible and sustainable products from EPS waste.

To integrate our vision into the circular economy of the UK and exhibit leadership both with demonstrated responsibility for our plastic waste streams and innovative product designs.
