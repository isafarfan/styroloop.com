Our Process

We take waste expanded polystyrene (EPS) and shrink the material down to its base plastic. With this we are able to create circular products, providing an end to end service, by offering both recycling and manufacture of sustainable plastic.

We take
EPS waste

Creating drop points or travelling to the origin of the waste.

We transform the EPS into DPS

Our process liberates the air bubbles that makes EPS so bulky. Shrinking the EPS to a 0.1% of its size. On site.

The resulting DPS can be moulded

Endless posibilities to create new products made of recycled EPS!
